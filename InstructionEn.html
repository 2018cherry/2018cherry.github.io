Important Notes for Participants

➢       Only registered runners will be allowed to participate. No transfers are allowed.

➢       The route of this race wanders around the mountains and thus would be more difficult than the race held in the metropolitans. All participants are expected to be trained and prepared for the Marathon and will be held responsible for their own health and ability to run the race.

➢       Participants are warned not to enter the race in poor state of health. Runners are advised to retire the race when detecting any sign of possible poor physical condition before and during the race.

➢       Emergency medical care will be available to deal with accidents during the race only, but the organizer is not liable for any further medical care.

Checking-in Information (For Overseas Runners NOT Dwelling in Taiwan)

➢       Time: 7:00 a.m., Feb. 1 (Sun), 2015.

➢       Location: New Taipei City Shuang-xi High School (Start Point)

➢       Please be sure to collect the Bib number and the T-shirt when checking in.

➢       Please be sure to attach the Bib number in front.

 

Baggage Check

➢       Time: 7:00 – 8:30

➢       Please fasten the exclusive baggage check strip (which will be available when checking-in) onto your baggage.

➢       Your bib number must be presented when claiming your baggage.

➢       Please be sure to pack your belongings in a single bag. Additional bags will not be accepted for deposit. 

➢       It is recommended not to put fragile and valuable items in your baggage. Please note that the organizer is not responsible for damage and loss of valuables.

 

Assembly of Runners to Start Line

Time: 8:20 – 8:30

Due to space constraint at the start line to ensure smooth start of the race, runners are advised to line up in areas corresponding to your estimated finishing time. The race staff will hold the banner with estimated finishing time at the assembly area.

 

Important Notes during the Race

➢       Runners should stay alerted, though there is little traffic through the whole course, except the lead vehicle. Please note also that escort vehicles might be on the course.

➢       Fluid stations will be set up along the course (6 stations).

➢       Taking alcoholic beverages during the race is prohibited.

➢       Runners who have not reached the turn around point 3.5 hours after start will be disqualified.

➢       Toilets are located at various points on the course (public and temporary toilet facilities). 

 

Important Notes after the Race and Finisher's Certificate

➢       You'll get the rank card, a finisher's medal with a bath towel at finish line.

➢       The organizer would provide lunch box after a runner finishes his or her race. Please fetch them with your bib number.

Weather Condition

Shuang-xi is located near the north-eastern coast of Taiwan. The weather here in winter is often cold (average 14 ℃) and humid. Rainy days with quite low temperature along the course are often expected. Proper clothing is highly recommended to keep warm before race starts.

 

For detail weather information in Taiwan, please refer to the web site of Central Weather Bureau of Taiwan:

http://www.cwb.gov.tw/eng/index.htm

(Please check the weather report for "Keelung North Coast".)
